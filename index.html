About Me

Hello and welcome to my website. This site is the result of my occasional enthusiasm for front-end development. It currently serves as a place to introduce myself and all the things about me which could be work-related.

I am a PhD student in Biochemistry with a Master degree in Chemistry. My interest in, well, pretty much everything IT-related was something I had already been aware of back when I was in school. I remember trying to teach myself C when I was about 13 years old, having a tough time with the math in the tutorial. Also, this was probably the reason why I put this area on hold for quite some time after that.

Anway, my interest in chemistry was at least as great back then. Eventually, I ended up studying chemistry and am working in this field ever since.

However, some time before the corona pandemic, my interest in the IT sector grew again. It started learning programming again by doing a Python tutorial via some app, but it was Harvard's CS50 course that finally got me started. I read books, did some more courses and started trying to program something of my own.

Now, I am constantly motivated to further educate myself in this area. For more infos about me, check out my other profiles on LinkedIn or GitHub below.
